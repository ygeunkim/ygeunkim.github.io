Young Geun Kim

Ph.D. in Statistics

Sungkyunkwan University (SKKU)

I received my Ph.D. in statistics from Sungkyunkwan University (SKKU) in 2025. My thesis was Bayesian Modeling and Forecasting of High Dimensional Long Range Dependent Time Series, advised by Changryong Baek. During PhD, I researched forecasting models based on Bayesian Vector Heterogeneous Autoregressive Models with Minnesota prior and Generalized Double Pareto shrinkage prior. I also developed a TS anomaly detection algorithm.
